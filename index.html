About Me

My name is Rihan, and I am currently pursuing my studies at the Manitoba Institute of Trade and Technology (MITT). As part of my program, I am gaining in-depth knowledge in web and software development, exploring various programming languages, and learning the full spectrum of skills required for modern development. Through this journey, I am becoming proficient in both front-end and back-end technologies, which will ultimately equip me to work as a full-stack developer. I am passionate about creating innovative digital solutions, and upon completing my degree, I look forward to applying my skills in building seamless, user-friendly applications that meet the needs of the ever-evolving tech industry.

This fascination led me to pursue my studies at the Manitoba Institute of Trade and Technology (MITT), where I could turn my passion into practical knowledge. By enrolling at MITT, I am not only fulfilling my childhood dream but also equipping myself with the skills and expertise to thrive in the ever-growing tech industry. This opportunity is allowing me to dive deeper into the world of technology and build a solid foundation for my future career.
